# OUTLIER CLOTHIERS - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**Problem**: Luxury goods have insane markups. Factories in China produce surplus that needs liquidation. Consumers want designer goods but can't afford retail prices.

**Solution**: We bridge the gap. Buy liquidation pallets from ViaTrading/B-Stock, pre-sell mystery boxes on Telegram, zero inventory risk.

**Market**: Crypto-native consumers on Telegram who understand arbitrage and love treasure hunt mechanics.

## The Unfair Advantage

### 1. Pre-Funded Model = Zero Risk
- Sell mystery boxes BEFORE buying pallets
- Only purchase once funding goal reached
- If drop fails, automatic refunds
- We never hold inventory we can't sell

### 2. Telegram-Native Distribution
- No Shopify fees (2.9% + $0.30)
- No payment processor fees (Stripe 2.9%)
- Stars/TON payments = near-zero fees
- Built-in viral distribution (group chats)

### 3. Transparent Manifest = Trust
- Show exact pallet source (ViaTrading link)
- Display manifest with retail values
- Real-time funding progress
- Community validates purchases

## Revenue Model

### Mystery Box Economics

**Example: Hugo Boss Pallet**
- Pallet Cost: $45,000 (10,000 units)
- Mystery Boxes: 100 @ $12 USD each
- Revenue: $120,000
- Profit: $75,000 (62.5% margin)
- Shipping: -$15,000 (est. $150/box)
- **Net Profit: $60,000 (50% margin)**

### Scaling Path

**Month 1-3**: 2-3 pallets/month @ $10K profit each = $30K/month
**Month 4-6**: 5-8 pallets/month @ $15K profit each = $90K/month
**Month 7-12**: 10-15 pallets/month @ $20K profit each = $250K/month

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk: Pallets don't meet quality expectations
**Mitigation**: Only buy manifested pallets with clear retail values. Start small. Build reputation slowly.

### Risk: Can't reach funding goals
**Mitigation**: Lower box prices or reduce funding targets. If truly unfundable, auto-refund (zero risk).

### Risk: Shipping/logistics costs eat margins
**Mitigation**: Partner with 3PL for bulk rates. Factor shipping into box prices. Canada → US cross-border optimized.

### Risk: Payment processor issues (crypto volatility)
**Mitigation**: Accept stablecoins (USDC/USDT). Use Coinbase Commerce for fiat on-ramp. Multi-payment support.
