## Visual Brand Guidelines

### Color Palette (Use in all posts)
- **Primary**: #D4AF37 (Champagne Gold)
- **Background**: #0A0A0A (Obsidian Black)
- **Accent**: #FFFFFF (Pure White)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Cinzel (all caps)
- **Body**: Inter
- **Accent Numbers**: JetBrains Mono

### Logo Usage
- Always use on black background
- Minimum size: 48px height
- Clear space: 1x logo height on all sides

### Post Aesthetics
- Dark luxury theme (black/gold)
- High contrast
- Minimal text overlays
- Focus on product imagery
- Use geometric shapes (circles, squares)

**Pro Tip**: Create templates in Canva with your brand colors/fonts. This makes daily posting 10x faster.
